POETRY

The Hope

May 25, 2022


Oh, I've seen it, seen it, seen it,

The bubbles of hope forming,

Oh, I've done it done it done it,

Bursted the bubbles of hope,

Oh, I've seen it, seen it, seen it,

The full moon in the clear sky,

Oh, I've done it, done it, done it,

Blocked my vision with hands,

Oh sun, show that there's a day,

I've seen enough moon,

I've seen enough night,

I can tolerate the heat,

Just show me the day!

Show me, show me, show me.

~Divyanshu Sharma
